# 📊 Project Visualizations

Welcome to the **Images** directory of the **Forecasting of Smart City Traffic Patterns** project.

This folder contains all visualizations generated during the **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** and **Machine Learning model evaluation** phases. These figures provide valuable insights into traffic behavior, feature relationships, and the predictive performance of the developed model.

---

# 📂 Visualization Index

## 🚦 1. Traffic Volume Over Time
**File:** `traffic_over_time.png`

Visualizes traffic flow over the entire observation period.

**Purpose**
- Identify long-term traffic trends
- Detect seasonal fluctuations
- Observe sudden traffic spikes
- Understand overall traffic growth

---

## ⏰ 2. Traffic by Hour
**File:** `traffic_by_hour.png`

Displays the average number of vehicles across different hours of the day.

**Insights**
- Peak traffic hours
- Off-peak traffic periods
- Daily traffic behavior
- Rush-hour identification

---

## 📅 3. Traffic by Day of Week
**File:** `traffic_by_day.png`

Compares average traffic volume across different days of the week.

**Insights**
- Weekday vs Weekend comparison
- Weekly traffic trends
- High and low traffic days

---

## 🛣️ 4. Traffic by Junction
**File:** `traffic_by_junction.png`

Illustrates traffic distribution across different city junctions.

**Insights**
- Busiest junctions
- Least congested locations
- Traffic variability between junctions

---

## 🔥 5. Correlation Heatmap
**File:** `correlation_heatmap.png`

Shows the correlation between numerical features used in the Machine Learning model.

**Purpose**
- Identify feature relationships
- Detect highly correlated variables
- Support feature selection

---

## 🎯 6. Actual vs Predicted Vehicles
**File:** `actual_vs_predicted.png`

Compares actual traffic volume with model predictions.

**Purpose**
- Evaluate prediction accuracy
- Visualize model performance
- Detect prediction errors

---

## 🌳 7. Feature Importance
**File:** `feature_importance.png`

Displays the contribution of each feature in the Random Forest Regression model.

**Insights**
- Most influential features
- Feature ranking
- Model interpretability
